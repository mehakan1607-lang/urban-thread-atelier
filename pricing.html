Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time without penalties. Your membership will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.

Do discounts apply to sale items?

Membership discounts apply to regular prices. Sale items have their own pricing structure. Combine them for maximum savings!

Gold and Black members get access to our styling team. Book your consultation through your account dashboard.
